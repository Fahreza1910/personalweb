At Glance

At Glance

Established in 2011, PT Golden Ocean Line is a leading shipping company under Indonesian Flag in dry bulk transportation services to cater the distribution activities nationally and internationally.

PT Golden Ocean Line continuously prioritizes customer satisfaction as a company that operates in the customer service field. Our professional work with exceptional service quality is our standard to provide excellent services.

PT Golden Ocean Line contributes to the national economic growth by providing logistic solutions in the national distribution system. To support these efforts, PT Golden Ocean Line is continuously adapting to technological developments with numerous innovations by our existing resources.

PT Golden Ocean Line also plays a vital role in supporting the sustainable supply chain through its advantages. We ensure that every business activity occurs transparently and professionally by relying on maximum resources.
